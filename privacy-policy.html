Privacy policy

Data collected

When you use the contact form we collect:

  • Your professional e-mail address
  • The information you type in the “Message” field
  • A hidden _gotcha field (anti-spam, not stored)

Purpose of processing

Data is used solely to answer your enquiry. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Legal basis

Legitimate interest (Article 6-1 f GDPR): B2B prospect management.

Retention period

Messages are kept for 12 months in our professional mailbox then deleted.

Your rights

To access, rectify or delete your data, e-mail dpo@altiora-ghs.com. We answer within 30 days.

Data hosting

E-mails are hosted by OVH (France). Formspree messages are routed to our mailbox and deleted from the Formspree dashboard within 30 days.
